Overview

Purpose

Provide and promote information about the services the hotel chain provides. The website will provide a way to make reservations and will include contact information.

Audience

The audience are members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints who are temple recommend holders and meet one or some of the following characteristics:

  • Need to travel long distances to participate in temple ordinances and may have to stay in a temple area over the night.
  • Parents who need someone to babysit their children while they participate in temple ordinances.
  • Families or groups of members that would like to have a meal or activity either before or after they participate in temple ordinances.

Typography

Heading Font: 'Playfair Display', serif

An elegant font that resembles the culture of the Church. Making it cursive makes it look even more elegant.

Paragraph Font: 'Raleway', sans-serif

This is not as elegant as 'Playfair Display', but it reminds me of the font used for the scriptures. Besides, this font is easier to read, which is helpful for paragraphs.

Normal paragraph example

We offer a complete package for whatever your needs are in relation to temple service and accomodations. Our shuttle runs to meet the endowment schedule and our babysitting servcies provide an afforable and safe alternative for your family.

Colored paragraph example

Plan on using our free changing room for you and your fellow patrons as you transition from traveling to the temple to actually entering the temple. We want you to feel comfortable changing and freshing up in order to focus on meaningful service. Let us take care of you.
